About Us

We are FIRST LEGO League Team 2244, Team Name! Lego robotics can be hard, but with time and effort it can be worth it. That's why with our team of 9, we were able to do what a team of 3 or 4 can do in double the time. We as a team are dedicated to hard work and celebrating our victories, and then going right back at it. We are a school team, so we get a lot less time, but are able to get stuff to work just as well.
